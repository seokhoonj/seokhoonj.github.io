테일러 급수 (Taylor Series)
---

다항함수
\\( f(x) = t\_0 + t\_1(x-a) + t\_2(x-a)^2 + t\_3(x-a)^3 + \ldots + t\_k(x-a)^k \\)

\\( f'(x) = t\_1 + 2t\_2(x-a) + 3t\_3(x-a)^2 + \ldots + kt\_k(x-a)^{t-1} \\)
